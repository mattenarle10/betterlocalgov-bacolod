# Barangays

Bacolod City has **61 barangays** organized into two congressional districts.

## District 1 (30 Barangays)

Alijis, Banago, Bata, Cabug, Estefania, Felisa, Granada, Handumanan, Mandalagan, Mansilingan, Montevista, Pahanocoy, Punta Taytay, Singcang-Airport, Sum-ag, Taculing, Tangub, Villamonte, and others.

## District 2 (31 Barangays)

Alangilan, Bacolod City Proper, Bangga Ciano, Bays, Capitol Subdivision, Dawis, Eastwood, Greenland, Homesite, Magsungay, Mandalagan, Matab-ang, Pacita, Pahanocoy, Purok Sigay, Riverside, San Antonio, San Juan, San Sebastian, Singcang, Sum-ag, Sunshine Valley, Tangub, Villamonte, Vista Alegre, and others.
