# Free Legal Assistance — Bacolod City

Indigent citizens can access free legal services through the **Public Attorney's Office (PAO)** and other legal aid programs.

---

## 1. Services Available

| Service              | Provider                               | Who Can Avail                                    |
| -------------------- | -------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------ |
| Legal consultation   | PAO, IBP, City Legal Office (referral) | Indigent citizens, low-income families           |
| Court representation | PAO                                    | Indigent persons in criminal, civil, labor cases |
| Document preparation | PAO, IBP                               | Affidavits, complaints, legal letters            |
| Barangay mediation   | Barangay Hall (Lupong Tagapamayapa)    | All residents (for minor disputes)               |

---

## 2. Eligibility

**Who Qualifies for Free Legal Aid:**

- Monthly income below ₱25,000 (individual) or ₱40,000 (family)
- Senior citizens, PWDs, solo parents, women, children
- Victims of domestic violence, trafficking, or abuse
- Persons charged with crimes punishable by 6+ years imprisonment

---

## 3. Requirements

| For                  | Requirements                                                                 |
| -------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Legal consultation   | Valid ID, documents related to your case                                     |
| Court representation | Certificate of Indigency (from Barangay or DSWD), valid IDs, court documents |
| Barangay mediation   | Valid ID, complaint details                                                  |

---

## 4. How to Apply

### For PAO Services:

1. Visit **PAO Regional Office VI** in Iloilo City (covers Negros Occidental)
2. For Bacolod inquiries: Contact **City Legal Office** or **DSSD** for referrals
3. Fill out application form
4. Interview with PAO lawyer
5. If accepted, lawyer will be assigned to your case

### For IBP Legal Aid:

1. Visit **IBP Bacolod Chapter Office**
2. Request free legal consultation
3. Volunteer lawyers provide advice and document preparation

### For Barangay Mediation:

1. Visit your **Barangay Hall**
2. File complaint with **Lupong Tagapamayapa**
3. Free mediation for disputes (required before court filing)
