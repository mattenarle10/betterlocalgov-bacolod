# Birth, Marriage, and Death Certificates — Bacolod City

The **Local Civil Registrar (LCR)** of Bacolod City handles civil registration services including birth certificates, marriage licenses, and death certificates.

---

## 1. Services Available

| Service                   | Where to Apply                                   | Requirements                                                         |
| ------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------ | -------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Birth certificate copy    | Local Civil Registrar or PSA Serbilis Center     | Valid ID, birth details (name, date, place)                          |
| Marriage license          | Local Civil Registrar (where either party lives) | Birth certificates, valid IDs, cedula, CENOMAR, barangay certificate |
| Marriage certificate copy | Local Civil Registrar or PSA Serbilis Center     | Valid ID, marriage details                                           |
| Death certificate copy    | Local Civil Registrar or PSA Serbilis Center     | Valid ID, death details                                              |
| Late registration (any)   | Local Civil Registrar                            | Affidavit of late registration, supporting documents, valid IDs      |

---

## 2. Fees

| Service           | LCR Bacolod | PSA Serbilis |
| ----------------- | ----------- | ------------ |
| Certificate copy  | ₱50-₱100    | ₱155         |
| Marriage license  | ₱150-₱300   | N/A          |
| CENOMAR           | N/A         | ₱210         |
| Late registration | ₱300-₱500   | N/A          |

**Note:** PSA copies are required for passports, visas, and overseas employment. Local copies are sufficient for local transactions.

---

## 3. How to Apply

### For Certificate Copies:

1. Visit **Local Civil Registrar Office** or **PSA Serbilis Center** (SM City Bacolod, Robinsons Place Bacolod)
2. Present valid ID and provide details of the certificate needed
3. Pay fee at cashier
4. Receive certificate (same day for LCR, same day to 3 days for PSA)

### For Marriage License:

1. Both applicants must appear in person at **Local Civil Registrar**
2. Submit all requirements (birth certificates, IDs, cedula, CENOMAR, barangay certificate)
3. Pay application fee
4. Wait for 10-day posting period
5. Marriage license valid for 120 days after issuance
