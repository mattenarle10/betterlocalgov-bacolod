# Community Tax Certificate (Cedula) — Bacolod City

The **Community Tax Certificate (CTC)**, commonly known as **cedula**, is a basic tax document required for various transactions.

---

## 1. What is a Cedula?

The cedula serves as:

- Proof of payment of community tax
- Required document for many government and private transactions
- Needed for notarization, business permits, marriage licenses, and other services

---

## 2. Who Needs a Cedula?

**Required for:**

- Individuals 18 years old and above with income
- Corporations, partnerships, and associations

**Common Uses:**

- Business permits and licenses
- Notarization of documents
- Marriage license application
- Job applications and employment contracts
- Court filings and legal documents

---

---

## 4. Requirements

**For Individuals:**

- Valid government-issued ID
- Proof of income (optional, affects tax amount): payslip, ITR, or affidavit of no income

**For Businesses:**

- SEC/DTI Registration Certificate
- Business Permit
- Financial statements or ITR
- Valid ID of authorized representative

---

## 5. Cedula Fees

### For Individuals

**Basic Community Tax:** ₱5.00 (minimum)

**Additional Tax:** ₱1.00 for every ₱1,000 of gross annual income (maximum ₱5,000)

**Examples:**

| Annual Income | Total Tax |
| ------------- | --------- |
| No income     | ₱5.00     |
| ₱50,000       | ₱55.00    |
| ₱100,000      | ₱105.00   |
| ₱250,000      | ₱255.00   |
| ₱500,000      | ₱505.00   |

### For Businesses

**Basic Community Tax:** ₱500.00 (minimum)

**Additional Tax:** ₱2.00 for every ₱5,000 of gross receipts or property value (maximum ₱10,000)

---

## 6. How to Apply

1. Go to **Barangay Hall** or **City Treasurer's Office**
2. Fill out CTC application form (provide personal info, income, occupation)
3. Present valid ID
4. Pay community tax at cashier
5. Receive Community Tax Certificate

**Processing Time:** Same day (5-15 minutes)

---

## 7. Validity and Renewal

- **Valid for 1 year** from date of issue
- Expires on **December 31** of the year issued
- Renew every January (same process as initial application)

## 9. Important Reminders

- Declare income honestly - false declaration is punishable by law
- Keep your cedula safe - you'll need it for many transactions
- Make photocopies for your records
- Renew every year (no penalty for late renewal, just get a new one)
