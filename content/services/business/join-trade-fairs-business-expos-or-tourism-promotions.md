Bacolod City supports local businesses through trade fairs, expos, and tourism promotion activities organized by various city departments.

---

## Where to Find Opportunities

### City Government Programs

- **Department of Local Economic Development and Investment Promotions**
- **Bacolod City Tourism Office**
- **Business Permits and Licensing Division (BPLO)**

### How to Stay Updated

- Follow [Bacolod City Official Facebook](https://facebook.com/bacolodcitygovernment)
- Check [bacolodcity.gov.ph](https://bacolodcity.gov.ph) for announcements
- Contact BPLO for business support programs

---

## Types of Events

- **Trade Fairs**: Local products and services showcase
- **Business Expos**: Networking and business opportunities
- **Tourism Promotions**: MassKara Festival and city events
- **Business Forums**: Training and development programs

---

## How to Participate

1. **Watch for announcements** on official channels
2. **Contact organizing department** for registration
3. **Submit requirements** (business permit, product info)
4. **Attend event** and promote your business
