# Bacolod City Government: Free Health Services

The Bacolod City Government offers free **check-ups, medicines, and vaccines** through the City Health Office (CHO) and barangay health centers.

---

## 1. Free Medical Check-ups

| Service              | Where                        | Schedule           | Requirements                                              |
| -------------------- | ---------------------------- | ------------------ | --------------------------------------------------------- |
| General consultation | City/Barangay Health Centers | Mon–Fri, 8 AM–5 PM | Individual Treatment Record (ITR) — issued on first visit |

---

## 2. Free Basic Medicines

| Service              | Where                    | Schedule           | Requirements                                      |
| -------------------- | ------------------------ | ------------------ | ------------------------------------------------- |
| Free basic medicines | City Health Office (CHO) | Mon–Fri, 8 AM–5 PM | Doctor's prescription and/or referral from clinic |
| Senior citizen meds  | City Health Office       | Pharmacy hours     | Must be an indigent senior citizen                |

---

## 3. Free Vaccines & Immunization

| Beneficiary       | Vaccine/Service                                          | Where                   | Schedule                        | Requirements                                           |
| ----------------- | -------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------- | ------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------ |
| Children          | BCG, Pentavalent, OPV/IPV, MMR, Hepatitis B, PCV13 (EPI) | Barangay Health Centers | Typically Wednesdays, 8 AM–4 PM | ECCD card (first-timers can get one onsite)            |
| Pregnant women    | Tetanus Toxoid vaccine + supplements                     | Barangay Health Centers | During prenatal services        | Proof of pregnancy, ECCD/record                        |
| Animal bite cases | Anti-rabies vaccine (first dose free)                    | City Health Office      | Clinic hours                    | Proof of Bacolod residency (e.g., voter's certificate) |

---

## How to Avail

1. **Check-ups** → Go to your Barangay Health Center or City Health Office; first-timers will be issued an ITR.
2. **Medicines** → Bring a doctor's prescription/referral; submit at CHO pharmacy.
3. **Child immunization** → Visit your barangay health center on immunization day with ECCD card.
4. **Pregnancy services** → Attend prenatal visits at the barangay health center for free vaccine and supplements.
5. **Anti-rabies** → Report to the City Health Office; bring proof of residency.
6. **Senior citizen medicines** → Visit City Health Office; follow program guidelines.
