# Go to the Local Hospital for Treatment or Confinement — Bacolod City

This guide covers **where to go**, **what to bring**, and **how admission works** at hospitals in Bacolod City.

---

## Public Hospitals

### City Health Office / Public Health Centers

- **Type**: Primary care, outpatient services
- **Location**: BBB Street, Bacolod City, 6100 Negros Occidental
- **Phone**: (034) 434-9122
- **Services**: General consultation, basic treatment, referrals

---

## Private Hospitals

Bacolod City has several private hospitals for more specialized care. Contact them directly for services and rates.

---

## What to Bring

### For Outpatient Consultation

- Valid ID
- PhilHealth card (if applicable)
- Previous medical records (if any)

### For Hospital Admission

- Valid ID
- PhilHealth card and MDR (Member Data Record)
- Previous medical records
- Referral letter (if from another facility)
- Cash or payment method for deposits

---

## How Admission Works

1. **Emergency** → Go directly to Emergency Room
2. **Scheduled Admission** → Coordinate with your doctor; bring requirements
3. **PhilHealth** → Present card and MDR at admission; file claims with hospital
