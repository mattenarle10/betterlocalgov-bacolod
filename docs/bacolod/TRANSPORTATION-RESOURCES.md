## üöï Public Transportation in Bacolod

### Jeepney Routes

Jeepneys are the primary mode of public transportation in Bacolod City. Routes cover all major areas and barangays.

**For updated routes:**

- Check [CommuteBacolod.com](https://www.commutebacolod.com/)
- Ask drivers for route information
- Look for route signage on jeepneys

### Taxis

Metered taxis are available throughout Bacolod City.

**Standard Rates:**

- Flag down rate: Check current LTO/LTFRB rates
- Per kilometer: Follow metered rates
- Available 24/7 in major areas

### Tricycles

Tricycles serve barangay and short-distance routes.

**Usage:**

- Fixed rates within barangay zones
- Negotiate fare for special trips
- Available in all barangays

### Inter-Island Transport (BREDCO - Iloilo to Bacolod Routes)

**BREDCO (Bacolod Real Estate Development Corporation)** operates the Banago Port for inter-island passenger and cargo services.

**Routes:**

- **Iloilo to Bacolod** (and vice versa)
- Regular ferry services
- Bus + Ferry combination packages available

**Banago Port:**

- Location: Banago, Bacolod City
- Services: Passenger terminal, cargo handling
- Main route: Connects to Iloilo City port

**For schedules and bookings:**

- Visit Banago Port directly
- Check with ferry operators (Weesam Express, FastCat, etc.)
- Book combination bus+ferry tickets for seamless travel

**Travel Time:** Approximately 1 hour by fast ferry (Iloilo-Bacolod)

## üí° Commute Tips for Bacolod

### Peak Hours

- **Morning:** 6:00 AM - 9:00 AM
- **Evening:** 5:00 PM - 8:00 PM
- Plan extra travel time during these hours

### Safety Tips

- Keep valuables secure
- Be aware of your surroundings
- Use well-lit stops at night
- Have exact fare ready for jeepneys

### Eco-Friendly Options

- Use public transport when possible
- Carpool with colleagues
- Consider biking for short distances
- Support sustainable transport initiatives
